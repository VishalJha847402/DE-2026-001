# Spark Series — Practice Questions Hub

> Every lesson has **10 hidden practice questions** (3 Easy · 4 Medium · 3 Hard) at the bottom of its `README.md`.
> Answers are hidden behind `▶ Answer` toggles — try each question first, then reveal.
> This page is the **index** — organized by Topic. Click any lesson to jump straight to its questions.

---

## How to use this for revision

1. Open a lesson below.
2. Scroll to the **Practice Questions** section.
3. Answer Easy → Medium → Hard **without** opening the toggle.
4. Then reveal and check.
5. Mark in your Google Sheet: ✅ passed all / 🔁 needs review.

Per DE-2026 Rule #12 — a topic is "done" only when you pass Easy + Medium + Hard without help.
